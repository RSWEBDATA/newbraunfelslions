Lions meet the needs of local communities and the world. The 1.4 million members of our volunteer organization in 206 countries and geographic areas are different in many ways, but share a core belief – community is what we make it.

Ways to Give

Lions around the world are united in our spirit of bringing healing and hope to those who are most in need. Through Lions Clubs International Foundation (LCIF), 1.4 million Lions in 210 countries are able to turn compassion into action. As the official charitable organization of Lions Clubs International, LCIF has awarded 12,701 grants totaling more than US$950 million since its founding in 1968. The generosity of Lions has provided most of this revenue. But, there are still many more people who are counting on Lions’ help.
